An intimate, multisensory performance
for 3 audience. A blindfold, headphones
and warm hands.

Inspired by the mother figures from
Czech and Germanic folktales, we
explore the idea of tenderness and care,
perception of the known and unknown
tickling the mind, and stories of the
earth: simple and plain.

This is a sensory adventure to interesting
places and inner discoveries through
tactile touches, smells, stories and above
all, imagination.

Practical Information

FORMAT: It follows a one-to-one
performance format, so each show is
for 3 audience.

LANGUAGE: Available in English,
German and Czech. Each audience
listens to the stories on headphones
and they choose the language they
prefer.

DURATION: 45 min.

We can perform 4-5 shows each day.
